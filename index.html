me


Hello and welcome to my website! I believe in the immense potential that mapping has to empower us, capture our imagination, and help us envision what is possible. I believe in the potential of people. Anyone can map. Mapping helps us understand ourselves, our communities, and the power we have when we come together. I strive in my work to hold to a commitment and reverence to the places, and people that I put on a map.
A better future is possible.
Solidarity forever.
- Luke

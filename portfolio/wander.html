Wander

-----
In Colorado I used to put on a scavenger hunt every year on my birthday for all my friends. This year in Madison I decided to bring it back in a way. This project was a combinaion of psychogeography, forest bathing, and a big ol' three-way birthday party.





What is this?:
An experiment in exploring Madison in a new way! When you arrive each person will receive a unique set of instructions. These instructions will guide you to explore, play, improvise, observe, create, follow your whims, collaborate, and WANDER with the goal of unlocking a new perspective of a familiar place.

What to bring:

Comfortable shoes
A bike if you're able to
A water bottle
Art supplies (optional)
Your inner child (mandatory)



(Tentative) Schedule of Events:

3pm: Arrive and receive instructions.
3:30pm: Go Wander!
6:00pm: Reconvene for dinner and sharing. Dinner provided (suggested donation plz)
Hang out and do some art through the evening!

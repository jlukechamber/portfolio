Raw Data Dreams

Luke Chamberlain


This Mapbox style is called Raw Data Dreams.
It is inspired by the artwork created for the album Raw Data Feel by Everything Everything.

I was immediately drawn to the surreal dream-like quality of these images.

The band's latest project was heavily inspired by AI. These images and some lyrics on the album were procedurally generated.

I loved all the color palettes from these 5 pieces, so I thought "why stick with just one!"

To try to capture that dream world quality I decided to have the color palette blend from one to another as you move to higher zoom levels.

I think the effect turned out very fun. I like to call the zoom levels where one palette is transitioning into another "palettetones" (like ecotones...get it?).
